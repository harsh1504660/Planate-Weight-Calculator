This website is a weight calculator that helps users determine
the weight of objects on different planets. The website
utilizes the principles of physics and uses the gravitational
force of different planets to calculate the weight of
objects. The website provides users with an easy-to-use
interface where they can input the weight of an object in
kilograms, select the desired planet, and obtain the weight of
the object on that planet. This website is a useful tool for 
science students, astronomers, researchers, and anyone 
interested in learning about the effects of gravity on objects 
in different parts of the universe.
        
    
To calculate the weight of an object on different planets,
we need to use the formula:

Weight = Mass x Gravity
                
Where:
                
Weight is the force exerted on the object by the gravitational
field of the planet.
Mass is the amount of matter in the object, measured in
kilograms.
Gravity is the acceleration due to gravity on the planet,
measured in meters per second squared.
The gravitational acceleration on each planet is different
due to variations in their mass, size, and distance from
the sun. Therefore, the weight of an object will be
different on each planet.
                
To calculate the weight of an object on a specific planet,
we need to know its mass and the gravitational acceleration
on that planet. We can use the below tables to
find the gravitational acceleration of different planets.

    • Venus: 0.91
        
    • Earth: 1.00
            
    • Mars: 0.38
                
    • Jupiter: 2.34
                    
    • Saturn: 1.06
                        
    • Uranus: 0.92
                            
    • Neptune: 1.19
                                
    • Pluto: 0.06

Mars, where the gravitational acceleration is about 3.71
